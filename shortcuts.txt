Shortcuts:
  General:
    Hold Ctrl: navigate by words
    Hold Shift: select instead of navigating

  VS Code:
    Alt + up/down: move line
    Shift + Alt + up/down: duplicate line

    Terminal:
      Right-click file in Explorer
        > Open in Integrated Terminal (instead of cd*10)
      Ctrl+Shift+C: copy
      Ctrl+Shift+V: paste
